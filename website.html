google

google is the Internet search engine. Google is a big American technology company best known for its search engine, which helps people find information on the internet quickly. It was founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin. Besides searching the web, Google offers many other services like Gmail for email, Google Drive for storing files, and Google Maps for navigation. The name "Google" comes from a math term that means a very large number, showing its goal to organize a lot of information. Today, Google is a major part of our online lives, helping us access and use information easily.
